Make pizza on grill

Home

This grilled pizza is topped with tomato sauce, tomatoes, black olives, roasted red peppers, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. Of course, you can use whatever toppings you like!

Ingredients

  • garlic
  • peppers
  • cheese
  • black olives
  • chopped tomatoes

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients and mixed together
  2. make a dough
  3. put the souce on the dough
  4. enter it on the oven
  5. wait 15min to 20min

VOILA! ready to be eat
